My SEO Approach

I believe SEO should be clear, measurable, and built on fundamentals that last—not shortcuts that risk penalties or become obsolete after the next algorithm update.

Here's how I approach every project:

  • Technical Foundation First: Before anything else, I fix crawlability issues, optimize Core Web Vitals (LCP, CLS, INP), ensure proper indexation, and clean up site architecture so search engines can easily understand your site.
  • Entity & Semantic SEO: I map your brand, products, and services as entities, not just keywords. This helps you rank for broader query variations and improves relevance in AI-powered search results.
  • User-First Content: Content needs to answer real questions, match search intent, and provide value. I focus on creating content that people actually want to read and that search engines confidently rank.
  • GEO & AEO Ready: With AI search growing (ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews), I structure content so it's easy for AI systems to cite and reference—expanding your visibility beyond traditional SERPs.
  • Transparent Communication: You'll always know what I'm working on, why it matters, and what results to expect. No jargon, no black-box tactics.
